Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef stroganoff is a dish originating in Russia. It is often served with egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz package egg noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 10.5oz can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sautee ground beef for 5-10 minutes until it has browned.
  2. Boil a large pot of water. Cook egg noodles for 7-9 minutes until tender but firm. Drain and set aside.
  3. Discard any fat from the beef and stir in condensed soup and garlic powder. Simmer for 10 minutes while stirring.
  4. Take beef off of heat. Combine with egg noodles, sour cream, salt, pepper, and stir.
